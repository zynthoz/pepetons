# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Diners in Quezon City and Metro Manila looking for high-quality Filipino food and signature dishes (like Kapalmuks). Event organizers, families, and corporate clients seeking catering, party trays, or booking the banquet hall.

## Product Purpose
The official marketing and informational website for Pepeton's Grill & Catering. Its success is measured by high-quality presentation of all menus, increasing online discoverability, generating direct catering/banquet inquiries, and building brand awareness.

## Brand Personality
Authentic, robust, and welcoming. Representing traditional Filipino grill culture with premium quality and community warmth.

## Anti-references
Flat, sterile, white corporate SaaS templates. Over-templated restaurant sites that lack imagery or character. Chaotic, text-heavy delivery aggregator platforms.

## Design Principles
1. Immersive Filipino Warmth: A premium dark-mode aesthetic utilizing deep charcoal-browns, oxblood reds, and Kinpaku gold accents, reflecting the physical restaurant ambiance.
2. Bold Visual Food-First: Centered display headlines, generous whitespace, and high-quality photography to make the signature Kapalmuks and menu items the heroes.
3. Frictionless Inquiry Flows: Simple, clear, and context-specific contact paths for catering, banquet bookings, and party trays.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA contrast compliance (especially for text on dark backgrounds), support for reduced motion preferences, keyboard-accessible navigation and forms, and clear mobile layout sizes (≥16px body font).
