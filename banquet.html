Private Events Venue

Our Banquet Hall

Celebrate your milestones in our cozy, fully-equipped private event hall located at Mother Ignacia Ave, Quezon City.

An Elegant, Cozy Atmosphere

Pepeton's Banquet Hall features warm ambient lighting, rich dark wood finishes, and flexible layouts customized to your theme. Our air-conditioned space is the perfect backdrop for intimate celebrations and corporate events.

What is Included in Venue Bookings?

  • 4-Hour Exclusive Use: Full private use of the hall with setup/ingress time.
  • Sound System & Projector: Access to speakers, mixer, microphones, and screens.
  • Basic Venue Styling: Stage design, centerpieces, and table runners.
  • Dedicated Event Staff: Events manager, coordinators, and uniformed waiters.

Venue Booking & Reservation Rules

To secure the banquet hall, we require a reservation deposit of 50% upon booking approval, with the balance settled on the event date.

Minimum Inclusions: Requires booking of Fiesta Package B or C catering packages for at least 50 guests.
