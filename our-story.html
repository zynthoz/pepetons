The Masterpiece

How Kapalmuks Was Born

Our signature creation, the famous Kapalmuks (crispy pork face), was born from a pursuit of the ultimate crunch. It requires precision, time, and absolute dedication to the craft.

01

Cure & Slow-Boil

Cleaned, cured with sea salt and local herbs, then slow-boiled for hours until exceptionally tender.

02

Dehydrate & Hang-Dry

Hung-dry to exhaustively remove moisture from the skin, ensuring it puffs perfectly during the final step.

03

High-Heat Flash Fry

Dropped into high-temp oil so the skin immediately blisters into a light, airy, crackling crunch.

04

Serve Hot & Crackling

Bite-sized chunks on a wooden platter, paired with our house-brewed native spiced vinegar dip.
