Our Heritage

The Story of Pepeton's

How a passion for hearty Filipino cooking and community roots built a Quezon City dining landmark.

A Family Legacy of Flavor

Pepeton's Grill & Catering opened its doors with a simple but powerful vision: to provide a place where families and friends can gather to celebrate life over generous portions of exceptionally cooked Filipino comfort food. Established in the heart of Quezon City, our location at 10 Mother Ignacia Ave has grown from a humble local neighborhood grill into a destination for diners across Metro Manila.

The name Pepeton is a tribute to our founder's culinary inspiration—combining family heritage, traditional cooking styles, and the warmth of Filipino hospitality. From day one, we committed to cooking without shortcuts, slow-braising our stews, hand-grilling our seafood over open flames, and treating every guest like a member of our own household.

How Kapalmuks Was Born

Every great restaurant has a signature creation, and for Pepeton's, that dish is the famous **Kapalmuks**. It was born out of a desire to create a showstopping dish that captured the crispy, savory indulgence of Filipino pork dishes (like crispy pata and lechon kawali) but offered something completely unique in texture and presentation.

After months of experimenting with preparation methods, boiling durations, and unique dry spices, our culinary team perfected a technique to prepare pig's head (pork face) so that the skin puffed into an incredibly light, blistered crisp, while the meat beneath remained tender, juicy, and rich. When we first served it on a rustic wooden platter, customers were amazed by both the dramatic presentation and the incredible crunch. It immediately became a sensation, drawing food lovers from all over the country.

Rooted in the Quezon City Community

For years, Pepeton's has been a trusted partner for our neighbors' milestones. We've watched families celebrate birthdays, christenings, graduations, and wedding reunions within our walls. Beyond our dine-in restaurant, we expanded into catering services and private banquet hosting, helping clients design memorable events with the same beloved food that defines our brand.

We invite you to sit at our tables, share a crispy Kapalmuks, sip a cold drink, and enjoy the authentic hospitality that has made Pepeton's a home away from home.
