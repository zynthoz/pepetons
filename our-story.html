Our Heritage

The Story of Pepeton's

How a passion for hearty Filipino cooking and community roots built a Quezon City dining landmark.

A Family Legacy of Flavor

Founded in the heart of Quezon City at Mother Ignacia Ave, Pepeton's Grill began with a simple vision: to bring families together over exceptionally cooked, honest Filipino comfort food. We commit to cooking without shortcuts—slow-braising our stews, hand-grilling seafood over open flames, and treating every guest like family.

How Kapalmuks Was Born

Every great restaurant has a signature creation. For Pepeton's, it is the famous Kapalmuks (crispy pork face). Born out of a desire to create a showstopping dish, our team perfected a multi-stage process—curing, slow-boiling, dehydrating, and flash-frying. The result is an incredibly light, blistered crisp skin with tender, juicy meat underneath, served on a rustic wooden platter with our spiced native vinegar.

Today, Pepeton's remains a trusted QC landmark for neighborhood gatherings, celebrations, and private banquet events. We invite you to sit at our tables, share a crispy Kapalmuks, and experience our warm Filipino hospitality.
