To Go Trays & Pickup

Party Food To Go

Host your gatherings with your favorite Pepeton's comfort food, prepared in convenient, high-capacity trays ready for pickup.

Ordering Guidelines

Ideal for birthdays, office potlucks, and small gatherings that want Pepeton's food without needing full buffet setup or waiters.

⏰ Lead Time: Please order at least two (2) days in advance of your desired pickup date.

Party Tray Offerings

trys are available in two sizes: Medium (serves 8-10) and Large (serves 15-20).

The Famous Kapalmuks Tray

₱1,300 (Regular Box)

Our signature crispy pork face dish, pre-chopped and packed in our signature thermal box with double servings of spiced native vinegar sauce. Serves 6-8.

Sizzling Pork Sisig Tray

Med: ₱950 | Lrg: ₱1,800

Wok-seared pork cheeks, onions, and red-green chilies, prepared without egg for durability during transport. Tossed with seasoning.

Pancit Bam-I Tray

Med: ₱850 | Lrg: ₱1,600

Savory Visayan noodle mix of stir-fried Sotanghon and Canton noodles with cabbage, liver, shrimp, and garlic oil topping.

Beef Kare-Kare Tray

Med: ₱1,400 | Lrg: ₱2,700

Tender beef tripe and brisket slow-simmered in a rich peanut sauce, served with eggplant, sitaw, banana heart, and our signature shrimp paste.

Chicken Inasal Tray

Med: ₱950 (10pcs) | Lrg: ₱1,850 (20pcs)

Classic Bacolod-style grilled chicken legs and thighs marinated in calamansi, lemongrass, and annatto (atsuete) oil.

Chopsuey Tray

Med: ₱800 | Lrg: ₱1,500

Crisp, wok-tossed mixed garden vegetables, quail eggs, pork belly bits, cooked in a light savory gravy sauce.

How to Place Your Order

We make ordering party food trays simple and convenient:

  1. Choose your trays: Browse the offerings above and note the sizes (Medium or Large).
  2. Call or message us: Reach us at least 2 days in advance via phone or social media.
  3. Settle payment: Confirm booking details and secure payment via bank transfer or GCash.
  4. Pickup at restaurant: Drive by our Mother Ignacia branch at your scheduled time. Your trays will be hot and sealed in boxes.
